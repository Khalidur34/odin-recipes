Mexican Pasta Recipe

Description

A simple mexico-inspired pasta recipe

Ingredients:

  • 400g Regular Penne Pasta
  • 250g Heavy Cream
  • 400g Italian Passata
  • 500g Diced Chicken Breast

Procedure

  1. Fry chicken in a pan until golden brown
  2. Fry some onion and garlic with a pinch of salt
  3. Add passata and cook thoroughly
  4. Add heavy cream
  5. Add spices of your choice. Mine was Italian Herbs Mix and some Blackpepper
  6. Add Chicken and Pasta in the pan
  7. Add generous amount of cheese and turn off the stove
  8. Mix thoroughly
  9. Serve with parsley
